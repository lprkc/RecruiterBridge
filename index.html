How it works

From resume stack to personalized feedback in three steps.

1
Paste the job description
Add the full job posting — title, responsibilities, and requirements. The more detail you provide, the more precise the feedback will be.
2
Upload up to 10 resumes
Drop in PDF, Word, or text files. RecruiterBridge parses each resume and scores it against the role's exact requirements.
3
Generate and export
Each candidate gets a personalized report highlighting their strengths, gaps, and a clear recommendation. Export as PDF or Word and send.
4
Use the contact list
Every candidate's name, email, match score, and document status appears in one ready-to-use send list.

Features

Built for the reality of modern recruiting.

RecruiterBridge gives every recruiter the tools to communicate with candidates the way they deserve — without adding hours to your week.

🎯
Job-matched analysis
Every feedback report is generated against your specific job description, not a generic scoring rubric. Candidates hear exactly why they do or don't fit.
📄
PDF and Word export
Export as a polished PDF to send directly, or a Word document you can edit and personalize before sending. Both formats include a professional layout.
📋
Send-ready contact list
Every session produces a complete contact roster with candidate names, emails, match scores, and document status — ready to copy into your ATS or email client.
⚡
Batch processing
Process up to 10 resumes at once. RecruiterBridge analyzes each one in parallel — a stack of 10 resumes takes the same time as processing one.
🤝
Constructive by design
Feedback is always written to be respectful and constructive — including for candidates who are not a match. Every person walks away with something useful.
🔒
No data retained
Resumes and job descriptions are processed and immediately discarded. Nothing is stored, indexed, or used for training. Your candidates' data stays private.
